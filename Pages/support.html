Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is a prepaid mobile recharge? +
A prepaid mobile recharge involves adding talk time, data, and/or SMS validity to your mobile number by paying in advance. The services are activated immediately after the recharge is successful.
How do I perform a prepaid recharge? +
You can perform a prepaid recharge by selecting your plan and completing the payment process through UPI, debit/credit card, or net banking via our website or app.
Can I recharge for any mobile operator? +
Yes, you can recharge any mobile operator, including Airtel, Jio, Vodafone Idea, and more. Choose your operator and plan during the recharge process.
What happens if I run out of balance before my plan expires? +
If you run out of balance, you will need to recharge again to continue using the services. You can choose a plan with more talk time, data, or validity based on your needs.

What is a prepaid transaction? +
A prepaid transaction involves paying in advance for services, such as mobile recharge or internet plans.
How do prepaid mobile recharges work? +
Prepaid mobile recharges allow users to top-up their balance before using mobile data, talk time, or SMS services.
Are there any hidden charges in prepaid transactions? +
Most prepaid transactions are straightforward, but some providers may charge processing fees or taxes depending on the transaction method.
How can I track my prepaid transaction history? +
You can track your prepaid transaction history through your service provider's app or website, or by contacting customer support.
